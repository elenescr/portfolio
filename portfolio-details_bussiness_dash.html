Business Performance Dashboard (Power BI)

This interactive dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of business performance, combining actual sales data with budgeted and forecasted targets. It highlights key performance indicators such as Total Sales, Budget, Variance, and Profit, offering insights into trends over time, customer behavior, and profitability across products, segments, and regions. The report includes:

  • Executive KPI summary
  • Sales vs Budget/Forecast analysis
  • Time series trends (monthly, seasonal patterns)
  • Customer distribution insights
  • Profit breakdown by segment and category
